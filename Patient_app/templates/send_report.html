Send Report to Doctor

Why are you sending the report to the doctor?

The doctor will use the report to predict the likelihood of the patient having Parkinson's disease. They will leverage an SVC model, a machine learning algorithm, to analyze the report's data. The predictions made by the model will guide the doctor in diagnosing the patient. This enables early detection and potential treatment planning based on the model's results.

What is SVC, and how does it help detect Parkinson's disease?

SVC (Support Vector Classifier) is a machine learning algorithm that helps classify data into distinct categories. In detecting Parkinson's disease, SVC is trained to differentiate between healthy and affected individuals using speech and movement data. By analyzing patterns in the report, SVC predicts whether the patient exhibits signs of Parkinson's disease, aiding in early diagnosis.
